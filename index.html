Had a late study night? Consumed a lot of caffeine? Can't sleep?
If you're worried about your recent caffiene consumption, this is the place to go.
Simply enter in the number of fluid ounces of each drink you consumed and your age.
This site will tell you if you should be worried or not.


On the graph, each X is 50 milligrams of caffeine.
The top side of the graph is the safe caffeine limit for your age range.
The bottom side of the graph is what you consumed.
